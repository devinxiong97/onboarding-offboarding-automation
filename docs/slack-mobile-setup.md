# How-to: Add a Slack Workspace to Your Mobile Device

This guide explains how to add an organization's Slack workspace to your mobile
device. Because some workspaces are configured primarily for desktop use, you
generate the mobile sign-in QR code from the Slack desktop app.

> Example documentation. Substitute the actual workspace name in production use.

## Instructions

1. Open **Slack** on your computer (desktop app).
2. Select the target workspace from the workspace drop-down list.
3. Go to **Sign in on mobile** — your personalized QR code will appear.
4. On your phone, open Slack and scan the QR code, following the on-screen prompts.

> Keep the desktop app open and signed in to the workspace while you scan. The QR
> code is tied to your active session and is personal to your account — generate it
> yourself and don't share it.
