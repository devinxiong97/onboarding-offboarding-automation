# Remote Employee Onboarding — Corporate Card & Laptop

How remote employees obtain their work laptop. Instead of shipping a
pre-configured device, remote hires purchase a laptop themselves using a
pre-loaded corporate-card account, then complete a remote setup session with IT.

> Fabricated example data. Names, emails, and contacts are placeholders.

## When to use

Any new hire whose work type is **Remote**. In-office hires receive a device
through standard provisioning instead.

## Process overview

| Step | Owner | What happens |
|------|-------|--------------|
| 1. Card invite | IT / Accounting | New hire receives a corporate-card invite at their email and sets up the account. |
| 2. Notify Accounting | New hire | After accepting, the hire contacts Accounting to have the card issued. |
| 3. Digital card | Accounting | A digital card, pre-loaded and scoped for a laptop purchase, appears in the account. |
| 4. Purchase | New hire | Hire buys the laptop from an authorized retailer using the card; keeps the receipt to submit. |
| 5. Setup | IT | IT schedules a remote session to enroll the device and finish setup before day one. |

> **Questions about the corporate card** — invites, issuance, or receipts — go to
> `accounting@example.com`.

## Email template — "Your laptop is on its way"

Send to the remote hire before their start date. Replace `[First Name]`.

```
Subject: Your Laptop Is on Its Way: Here's What to Expect

Hi [First Name],

Welcome to the team. Before your first day, we want to make sure you have
everything you need to hit the ground running, starting with your laptop.
Here's how the process works for remote employees:

1. Card Invite — You'll receive an invite to our corporate-card platform at
   this email address. Please accept it and set up your account.
2. Notify Accounting — Once you've accepted, contact Accounting to have your
   card issued. Questions: accounting@example.com.
3. Your Digital Card — A digital card pre-loaded for a laptop purchase will be
   waiting in the platform. Nothing to do with it until you're ready to buy.
4. Purchasing — Buy the laptop from an authorized retailer using the card at
   checkout. Keep your receipt to submit after the purchase.
5. Setup — Once you have it, I'll schedule a remote session to enroll the
   device and get you ready for day one.

Questions along the way? Just reach out.

Welcome aboard,
IT Team
```
